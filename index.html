'Kraftstoff' (Fuel) offers you an uncluttered and elegant way to track your fuel economy.

Cars

  • Supports multiple vehicles.

Fill-Up

  • On each refuel simply enter distance driven, fuel unit price and amount of fuel.
  • Fuel data can be added and edited afterwards.
  • Partial fill-ups are supported (the data is settled on next full fill-up).

Graphical Display

  • Select a car and turn your iPhone into landscape mode to get interactive history charts for milage, fuel cost per liter/gallon and km/mile as well as a table with interesting statistical values.

Further Features

  • Export of your data via email as attachment. The data is exported in the simple and human-readable CSV-format.
  • Reimport of exported data is possible by sending CSV attachments in Mail.app via 'Open in' to kraftstoff.

Requirements

Kraftstoff requires an iPhone or iPod Touch running iOS 7 or newer.
