Kraftstoff by m-schmidt

'Kraftstoff' (Fuel) offers you an uncluttered and elegant way to track your fuel consumption.

Cars: - Supports multiple vehicles Fill-Up: - On each refuel simply enter distance driven, fuel unit price and amount of fuel - Fuel data can be added and edited afterwards - Partial fill-ups are supported (the data is settled on next full fill-up) Graphical Display: - Select a car and turn iPhone into landscape mode to get history charts for milage, fuel cost per liter/gallon and km/mile Further Features: - Export of your data as email (CSV attachment) - Reimport data from CSV file
