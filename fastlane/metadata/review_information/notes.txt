To fill the app with some demonstration data do the following:

- select the "Cars"-tab
- hit the "+" button on the upper right to add a new car
- enter "Apple" (without the quotes) as name for the car
- enter "DEMO" (without the quotes) as number plate
- hit "Done" on the top right

=> a new car (Toyota iQ+ with 52 data sets) is generated

To see the graphical statistics:

- select this newly created car
- rotate the device into landscape mode
